Transforming the data

1. Click on Transform Data
2. Make sure that the id, age, hypertension, heart_disease, stroke columns data type is Whole Number and Avg_glucose_level is Decimal Number.

Adding three new columns: Bmi Group, Age Group, Glucose Level Group

3. Click on the ‘Bmi’ column, then the replace value icon on the ribbon. Replace N/A with 0.
4. Change the ‘Bmi’ column data type to decimal number.

5. On the ribbon, click on add column, select conditional column, then Format as the screenshots below shows

Bmi = > 60 then 60-above
Bmi = > 50 then 50-59
Bmi = > 40 then 40-49
Bmi = > 30 then 30-39
Bmi = > 20 then 20-29
Bmi = > 10 then 10-19
Bmi = > 1 then 1-9
Else N/A

6. Age Group column

Age = > 55 then 55-70

Age = > 16 then 16-35
Else 15-below

8. Rename columns.
gender --> Gender
ever_married --> Marital Status

Residence_type --> Residence Type

Click on the Close and Apply button to save.
