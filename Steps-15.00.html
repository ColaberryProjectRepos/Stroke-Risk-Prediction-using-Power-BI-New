Creating Refresh date Table

Click on Get Data, Click on Blank Query, write the formula for Refresh Date in the formula bar.
= DateTime.LocalNow()
Click on Convert to Table.
Rename the Column name to “Refresh Date.”
Rename the Query name to “Refresh Date.”
Click on the Close and Apply button to save.
